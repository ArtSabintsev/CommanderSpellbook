Want to Submit a Combo?
At this time, combo submissions (and updates) are only being accepted via Discord, as we've learned from experience that many of them require a discussion. If your combo isn't listed here, or if you've found a bug with one of our combos, please join us on Discord to contribute to the catalog.

Search Tips
  • Type in the name of a single card, a Combo ID, or a Combo Result (see table below) to search the database.
  • Use the Color Filter bar to search for combos in a specific color combination.
  • Use the Card Filter bar to search for combos in with a specific amount of cards.
Combo Result
Definition
Draw the game Combo results in the game ending in a draw.
Exile all opponents' libraries Combo results in exiling all the cards in all of your opponents libraries.
Infinite colored mana Combo has the potential of producing infinite mana of all colors.
Infinite colorless mana Combo produces infinite colorless mana.
Infinite copies Combo produces infinite copies of a spell.
Infinite damage Combo results in dealing infinite damage to creatures and/or players.
Infinite death triggers Combo results in an infinite amount of death triggers on the stack.
Infinite ETB Combo results in an infinite amount of Enter the Battleground triggers on the stack.
Infinite Life drain Combo results in a player drainging opponents down to 0 life.
Infinite Lifegain Combo results in a player gaining infinite life.
Infinite Loot Combo results in an infinite amount of draw and discard effects.
Infinite LTB Combo results in an infinite amount of Leave the Battleground triggers on the stack.
Infinite mill Combo causes a player to place all of the cars in their librarary into their graveyard.
Infinite sac triggers Combo results in an infinite amount of sacrifice triggers on the stack.
Infinite scry Combo results in an infinite amount of scry triggers on the stack.
Infinite storm count Combo results in a player casting an infinite amount of spells in a given turn.
Infinite turns Combo results in infinite turns for the player.
Win the game Combo results in you instantly winning the game due to an alternate win condition.
About
The Commander Spellbook project exists to catalog all reasonable Commander/EDH combos and to make them easily available across all modern digital platforms.

The data powering the website, and the source code for the website itself, are completely free and open source under the MIT License. Simply provide us credit on your app if you consume the spreadsheet and/or API.

We encourage you copy this data so that it lives on!
